About the Course

Software System Development is a graduate-level course equipping students with a comprehensive understanding of the principles, tools, and practices involved in **designing and building modern software systems**. The course emphasizes a systems-oriented perspective, covering foundational topics like Operating Systems, Computer Networks, Software Engineering methodologies, and Database Systems (Relational and NoSQL).

Students will gain practical experience with industry-relevant technologies including Bash scripting, SQL, NoSQL databases, HTML, CSS, JavaScript, front-end frameworks, RESTful APIs, Python, and Flask. This course bridges theory and practice, preparing students to contribute effectively to complex software development projects.
